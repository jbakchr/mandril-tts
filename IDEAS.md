# 🎭 mandril-tts — Ideas

This document is a dumping ground for weird, fun, and experimental ideas.

No idea is too small, too stupid, or too chaotic.

This is not a roadmap.
This is not a plan.

It’s just:

> “What would be fun to hear out loud?”

---

## 🧠 How to use this

- Add ideas as they come — don’t overthink it
- Keep entries short and rough
- It’s okay if most of these never get built
- The goal is to explore _possibilities_, not commit to them

---

## 📝 Idea Template

Copy this when adding a new idea:

```markdown
### 🌀 Idea: [Short name]

**Description**

- What is this?
- What would it sound like?

**Example**

> Example text that would be spoken

**Voice / Style**
- calm / dramatic / chaotic / monotone / etc.
- any specific inspiration?

**Why it's fun**
- what makes this interesting or funny?

**Notes (optional)**
- technical thoughts
- weird edge cases
- anything to remember later
```

## 💡 Ideas

### 🌀 Idea: Overly dramatic narrator

**Description**

- Everything is read like a serious documentary
- Even completely trivial sentences

**Example**

    Jeg har købt en liter mælk.

**Voice / Style**

- slow
- deep
- dramatic pauses

**Why it's fun**

- contrast between content and delivery

---

### 🌀 Idea: Completely calm chaos

**Description**

- Voice is perfectly calm
- Content is increasingly absurd

**Example**

    Jeg har placeret en and i opvaskemaskinen. Det føles korrekt.

**Voice / Style**

- monotone
- emotionless

**Why it's fun**

- mismatch between tone and meaning

---

### 🌀 Idea: Passive-aggressive Danish

**Description**

- Sounds polite, but slightly annoyed

**Example**

    Det var da interessant, at du først svarer nu.

**Voice / Style**

- soft
- polite
- subtle tension

**Why it's fun**

- recognizable real-world tone 😄

---

### 🌀 Idea: Escalating chaos mode

**Description**

- Starts normal
- Gets more and more chaotic over time

**Example**

    Hej.
    Hej igen.
    HEJ!!!
    JEG ER HER STADIG!!!

**Voice / Style**

- increasing intensity

**Why it's fun**

- progression over time

---

### 🌀 Idea: Whispered conspiracy voice

**Description**

- Sounds like someone telling a secret

**Example**

    De holder øje med dig... også når du laver kaffe...

**Voice / Style**

- quiet
- intimate
- slightly paranoid

**Why it's fun**

- eerie but funny
